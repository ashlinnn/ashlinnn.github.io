About Me

Hi, my name is Ashley Lin! Some of you might know me as Ashley Miso, Ash, or Ashlin. I'm a 3rd year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley pursuing a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS). I enjoy learning how things work and understanding even the smallest details and mechanics of a process. My primary interests lie in integrating artificial intelligence with digital signal processing. You can find a wide range of my software and hardware projects here.

Teaching

I am also an Undergraduate Student Instrctor for the Electrical Engineering course (EE16B). Teaching is such a rewarding experience and through it, I have developed many unforgettable relationships. This will be my 3rd semester as part of course staff but I have been with the series since fall of my freshman year. My responsibilities include lecturing about lab content, debugging circuits, managing the grading infrastructure, training lab assistants, proctoring exams, and developing test material. I also coordinate with Texas Instrunments to plan the competition for featuring the SIXT33N final car project.
